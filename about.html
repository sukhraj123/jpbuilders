About Us

JP EcoBuilders Ltd. is a property investment and development company based in the Lower Mainland of BC. We primarily renovate, retrofit, and upgrade residential and commercial properties based on our core values of safety, comfort, and sustainability.


The EcoBuilders are defined by our high standards for quality and creative designs, leaving no stone unturned to ensure that each and every expectation of consumers is met or exceeded. We use eco-friendly, premium materials where possible and believe in repairing and restoring what is plausible, to reduce waste and promote health in the community.
